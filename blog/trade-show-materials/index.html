Trade Show Materials Guide

Everything you need to print for a successful trade show presence.

Your Trade Show Print Checklist

Booth Graphics

  • Retractable banner stands (most popular — portable, professional, reusable)
  • Pop-up display backdrop (10' wide for standard booth spaces)
  • Table throw or fitted cover with logo
  • Hanging banners or overhead signs (for larger spaces)

Handout Materials

  • Brochures or sell sheets (your key takeaway piece)
  • Business cards (bring more than you think)
  • Product catalogs or spec sheets
  • Promotional postcards with special show offers

Supporting Materials

  • Name badges or lanyards (custom printed)
  • Floor graphics to guide traffic to your booth
  • Presentation folders for qualified leads
  • Feedback forms or sign-up sheets

Timeline

Start planning print materials 4-6 weeks before your event. This allows time for design, proofing, production, and shipping. Rush options are available, but planning ahead ensures the best results and pricing.
