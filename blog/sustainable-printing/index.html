Sustainable Printing

Eco-friendly printing options that maintain professional quality.

Printing Can Be Green

Environmental responsibility and print quality aren't mutually exclusive. Today's sustainable printing options deliver results that match or exceed conventional alternatives.

Eco-Friendly Options

  • Recycled papers: Available in a wide range of weights and finishes. Modern recycled stocks are virtually indistinguishable from virgin paper
  • FSC-certified paper: Sourced from responsibly managed forests. Look for the FSC logo on your materials
  • Soy-based inks: Lower VOC emissions, easier to recycle, excellent color reproduction
  • Digital printing: Less waste than offset — no plates, less setup waste, print only what you need
  • Right-sizing print runs: Print the quantity you'll actually use. No excess going to recycling
  • Waterless printing: Eliminates water and chemical-laden fountain solutions from the process

What You Can Do

  • Choose recycled or FSC-certified papers for your projects
  • Opt for digital printing for shorter runs to minimize waste
  • Use standard sizes to reduce paper trim waste
  • Design for longevity — materials that stay relevant longer reduce reprint frequency
  • Include recycling symbols and "printed on recycled paper" on your materials
