Our Process

Simple, transparent, and focused on getting it right the first time.

1️⃣

Request a Quote

Call, email, or visit. Tell us what you need and we'll provide a detailed quote — usually same day.

2️⃣

Submit Your Files

Send us your print-ready files or work with our design team to create them. We accept all major formats.

3️⃣

Review Your Proof

We send a free digital proof for your review. Check every detail — we don't print until you approve.

4️⃣

Production

Your project goes to press. Expert operators ensure color accuracy, registration, and quality on every sheet.

5️⃣

Finishing

Cutting, binding, folding, laminating — whatever your project needs, done with precision.

6️⃣

Delivery

Free local delivery throughout San Diego, or pick up at our Poway location. On time, every time.
