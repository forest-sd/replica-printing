Mailing Services

Complete direct mail solutions — from print to mailbox.

Print It. Mail It. Done.

Direct mail remains one of the most effective marketing channels — with response rates 5-9x higher than email. Replica Printing handles the entire process: printing, addressing, sorting, and delivery to the USPS, so you can focus on your business.

What We Offer

  • Variable Data Printing: Personalized names, addresses, and content on each piece
  • Address Processing: CASS certification, NCOA updates, deduplication, and postal presorting
  • Bulk Mail Preparation: USPS-compliant sorting and bundling for maximum postage discounts
  • Every Door Direct Mail (EDDM): Reach every address on a carrier route — no mailing list needed
  • Postcards, Letters, Flyers: Any format, any quantity, full-color
  • Mailing List Services: Targeted list acquisition by geography, demographics, and more
