Binding & Finishing Services

The finishing touches that make your printed materials look polished and professional.

Professional Finishing for Every Project

The difference between a stack of pages and a professional document is in the finishing. We offer a complete range of binding and finishing options to give your materials the look, feel, and durability they deserve.

Binding Options

  • Coil Binding: Durable plastic coil — lays flat, 360° fold-back, ideal for manuals and reference guides
  • Comb Binding: Easy to add or remove pages, professional appearance, cost-effective
  • Perfect Binding: Glued spine with cover wrap — gives a book-quality, polished look
  • Saddle-Stitch: Stapled spine for booklets, programs, and thin catalogs
  • Wire-O Binding: Double-loop wire for a premium, modern look
  • Three-Ring Binder Inserts: Hole-punched pages with custom divider tabs

Finishing Services

  • Laminating (gloss or matte, pouches or roll)
  • Folding (letter, Z-fold, gate, roll, custom)
  • Cutting and trimming to custom sizes
  • Scoring and perforating
  • Padding (notepad creation)
  • Collating and assembly
  • Tab cutting and insertion
